About StoryCast

Amplifying voices, breaking barriers, and making storytelling accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

StoryCast was founded on a simple yet powerful belief: every story deserves to be heard, and every person deserves access to those stories, regardless of their abilities or circumstances.

We create and curate compelling audio and video content that celebrates diverse voices while ensuring that accessibility is never an afterthought—it's built into everything we do from the ground up.

What We Do

We produce original storytelling content across multiple formats, from intimate audio podcasts to visual documentaries, always with a focus on:

  • Highlighting underrepresented voices and perspectives
  • Ensuring complete accessibility for all audiences
  • Maintaining the highest standards of audio and video quality
  • Building an inclusive community of storytellers and listeners

Our Core Values

Inclusivity

We believe that everyone should be able to access and enjoy storytelling content, regardless of visual, auditory, cognitive, or motor abilities.

Quality

We're committed to producing high-quality content that respects our audience's time and intelligence while remaining fully accessible.

Diversity

We actively seek out and amplify stories from diverse communities, cultures, and perspectives that are often underrepresented in mainstream media.

Innovation

We continuously explore new technologies and techniques to improve accessibility and enhance the storytelling experience for all users.

Accessibility Commitment

At StoryCast, accessibility isn't a feature—it's a fundamental principle that guides every decision we make.

We are committed to meeting and exceeding WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards to ensure that our platform is usable by the widest possible audience, including people with disabilities.

Accessibility Features

📝 Captions and Subtitles

All video content includes accurate, synchronized captions. We provide captions in multiple languages where possible, ensuring that both deaf and hard-of-hearing users, as well as non-native speakers, can fully engage with our content.

📄 Complete Transcripts

Every audio and video story includes a full, downloadable transcript. Transcripts are available in searchable text format, making content accessible to screen reader users and those who prefer reading to listening.

⌨️ Keyboard Navigation

Our entire website is fully navigable using only a keyboard. All interactive elements can be accessed with the Tab key, activated with Enter or Space, and include visible focus indicators so you always know where you are on the page.

🎯 Screen Reader Support

We use semantic HTML and proper ARIA labels throughout our site to ensure compatibility with screen readers like JAWS, NVDA, and VoiceOver. All images include descriptive alt text, and complex elements are properly labeled.

🎨 Color Contrast

All text on our site meets WCAG AA contrast requirements (minimum 4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text). We never rely on color alone to convey important information, ensuring content is accessible to users with color blindness.

📱 Responsive Design

Our platform works seamlessly across all devices and screen sizes. Whether you're using a desktop computer, tablet, or smartphone, content adapts to provide an optimal viewing and listening experience.

🔊 Audio Descriptions

Video content includes optional audio description tracks that narrate important visual elements, making visual storytelling accessible to blind and low-vision users.

⚙️ Accessible Media Players

Our custom media players include accessible controls, keyboard shortcuts, and clear labeling. Users can easily adjust playback speed, enable captions, and navigate content using standard media control keys.

Standards & Compliance

StoryCast strives to conform to the following accessibility standards:

  • WCAG 2.1 Level AA - Web Content Accessibility Guidelines
  • Section 508 - U.S. Rehabilitation Act standards
  • ADA Title III - Americans with Disabilities Act compliance
  • EN 301 549 - European accessibility requirements
